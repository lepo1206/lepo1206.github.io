My Professional Experiences

Workday Internship

During the summer of 2023, I worked as a Software Developement Engineering intern for Workday. I was part of a team developing front-end tools and features for a widely-used service provided by the company. Development was primarily done with React.js, which I hadn't had experience with beforehand, but throughout my internship I quickly became proficient with it and finished all my assigned tasks.

FourWords Senior Capstone Project

Throughout my college senior year, I was working alongside a small team and the company Curve10 to develop a short-form social media application. I was part of the front-end section of our team, and was tasked to develop the user interface of the application using React Native, as well as integrating database endpoints with JavaScript.

The group picture in my "About Me" page is from the showcase expo our team took a part in for this project!

MACLab Research Through CU SPUR

During the summer of 2022, I worked alongside the MACLab research group on CU Boulder campus, as a part of CU SPUR (Summer Program for Undergraduate Research). I was part of a small team, where we created a website to showcase the lab's research on soft robotics, primarily using WordPress. I developed multiple interactive applets such as 3D model showcases and charts using JavaScript and various libraries, as well as designed the graphics used on the site's overlay.
